Mirador Heritage and Eco Park

Baguio City is a popular tourist destination in the Philippines, known for its cool climate, pine-dominated landscapes, and romantic atmosphere. One of the newest attractions in the city is the Mirador Heritage and Eco Park, located on top of Mirador Hill and near the famed Lourdes Grotto. This 5-hectare park is filled with many Instagrammable spots and offers a bamboo forest, a Japanese torii gate, and a view of the city.

Mirador Heritage and Eco Park is a great place to visit if you're looking for a new attraction in Baguio City. It offers a unique blend of nature, culture, and history that is sure to appeal to a wide range of travelers.
