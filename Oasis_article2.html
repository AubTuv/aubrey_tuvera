BAGUIO COUNTRY CLUB

Welcome to Baguio Country Club, a luxurious 5-star mountain resort nestled in the breathtaking city of Baguio, Philippines. With a rich history dating back to 1905, this prestigious club has been a haven for the elite and influential names in Philippine society. Immerse yourself in the charm of our world-class amenities and services, including our well-maintained 18-hole golf course, perfect for golf enthusiasts. Experience our commitment to sustainable environmental practices, which has earned us the 4th ASEAN Environmentally Sustainable Cities Award in 2017. Discover our impeccable conference facilities, ideal for hosting corporate events and gatherings. Join us in our social and civic activities, as we strive to make a positive impact on our community. Come and experience the epitome of luxury and elegance at Baguio Country Club.
